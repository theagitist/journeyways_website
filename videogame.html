The Digital Journey Begins

After an extensive design and conceptualization phase, the JOURNEYWAYS videogame is officially under active development.

This digital version carries the same commitment to identity exploration and collaborative narrative into an interactive medium, allowing players to participate regardless of geographical location.

Further updates will be shared as development progresses.
